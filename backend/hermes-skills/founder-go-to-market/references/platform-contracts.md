# Platform draft contracts

- X: concise hook, concrete insight, honest build context, one CTA; optional thread with one idea per post.
- LinkedIn: founder observation, specific problem, evidence or lesson, product connection, conversational CTA.
- Reddit: community-first title, transparent affiliation, useful standalone detail, no manufactured urgency.
- Product Hunt: clear tagline, maker story, problem, differentiator, launch offer only if real, feedback request.
- Hacker News: direct technical/product explanation, constraints and tradeoffs, no marketing superlatives.
