# Founder Go-to-Market

Produce an executable strategy and truthful platform drafts. Drafting is allowed; publishing requires explicit approval and a separate tool receipt.

## Procedure

1. Define the narrow initial audience, urgent job, current alternative, promise, proof, and conversion event.
2. Use the `RUNTIME LINKUP EVIDENCE` supplied immediately before the run to validate communities, competitors, platform norms, and audience language.
3. Choose at most three channels using reach, intent, founder advantage, cost, and feedback speed.
4. Design a two-week sequence of measurable experiments with owner, asset, CTA, metric, stop condition, and learning goal.
5. Draft platform-native posts only for selected channels. Adapt tone and structure; do not cross-post identical copy.
6. Produce `gtm_strategy` and `social_posts` artifacts.

## Guardrails

- Never fabricate traction, testimonials, scarcity, partnerships, community membership, or customer outcomes.
- Mark comparative claims and numbers for Founder approval.
- Respect community rules and identify promotional posts honestly.
- Do not publish, message, spend, or scrape personal data.
- Favor direct learning and qualified conversations over vanity impressions.

## Verification

Every channel must have evidence or a clear test rationale. Every experiment must have one measurable success criterion. Every post must contain a truthful CTA and no unsupported claim.
